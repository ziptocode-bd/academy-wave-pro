About This Project

Darpan Academy

Darpan Academy is an online learning platform built for Bangladeshi students — SSC, HSC, admission preparation and more. Founded by Md Ashikuzzaman, the platform offers structured courses, live classes, and a real-time exam engine. Batches include OneShot, Tesla, and Admission — designed for focused, fast-track preparation.

The complete web application — from UI design and authentication to the course enrollment system, exam engine, admin panel, and PWA setup — was designed and developed by Ridoan and OnonnoBit.
